# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis (Task 2)

## 🔍 Objective

Analyze customer data to identify churn patterns, retention drivers, and customer lifetime trends.

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib

## 📈 Key Insights

1. Around 26.5% of customers have churned.
2. Customers with month-to-month contracts have the highest churn (~43%), while long-term contracts have very low churn.
3. Customers who churn have lower tenure compared to retained customers.
4. Customers with higher monthly charges are more likely to churn.

## 💡 Recommendations

* Encourage long-term contracts using discounts or benefits.
* Improve early customer experience to reduce initial churn.
* Review pricing strategy for high-paying customers.
* Provide loyalty offers to retain customers.
